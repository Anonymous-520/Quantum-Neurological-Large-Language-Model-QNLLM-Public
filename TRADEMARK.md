## Violations & Consequences

### What Constitutes Infringement

**Trademark infringement includes:**

- Using confusingly similar marks in commerce
- Creating likelihood of confusion with consumers
- Diluting the distinctiveness of the mark
- Registering the mark as a domain or trademark
- Using the mark to suggest false endorsement

### Consequences of Infringement

**Unauthorized trademark use may result in:**

**Civil Litigation:**
- Lawsuit for trademark infringement
- Unfair competition claims
- Federal court action

**Injunctive Relief:**
- Court order to stop use immediately
- Temporary restraining orders
- Permanent injunctions

**Monetary Damages:**
- **Actual damages** - Sillionona's lost profits
- **Statutory damages** - Up to $2,000,000+ per counterfeit mark
- **Treble damages** - 3x actual damages for willful infringement
- **Attorney's fees** - Infringer pays Sillionona's legal costs

**Account & Platform Actions:**
- Removal from social media platforms
- Deletion of trademark-infringing accounts
- Banning from communities and forums
- Removal of infringing content from websites

**Domain Actions:**
- UDRP (Uniform Domain-Name Dispute-Resolution Policy) action

- DNS blocking
